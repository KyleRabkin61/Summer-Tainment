What I Did This Summer . . .

I was a counsler for the Greg Carter Hockey Camp. I started attending this camp when I was around eight years old and since then I continued to go to these camps. However, for this year, instead of being a student, I was rather a teacher and volunteered as a coach for the camp.

What is Greg Carter?

Greg Carter is a European Hockey School day camp that develops the skills of skaters. The camp is formatted fopr a four day camp, and each day introduces a new skill set for the skaters to learn. Each day also has five single hour sessions which tackle specfic skills for the skaters to grasp.

What Do You Learn in Greg Carter?

Greg Carter not only teaches the fundementals of the game and how to play, but also offers a variety of skills to the players. These skills include but are not limited to:

  • Skating
  • Shooting
  • Edge Work
  • Balance
  • Stick Handling
