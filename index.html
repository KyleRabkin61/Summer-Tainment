What is Greg Carter?

Greg Carter is a European Hockey School day camp that develops the skills of skaters. I started attending this camp when I was around eight years old and since then continued to go to these camps. However, for this year, instead of being a student, I was rather a teacher and volunteered as a coach for the camp.

What Do You Learn in Greg Carter?

Greg Carter not only teaches the fundementals of the game and how to play, but also offers a variety of skills to the players. These skills include:

  • Skating
  • Shooting
  • Edge Work
  • Balance
  • Stick Handling
